Requirements
Who can donate blood?

Age

You must be aged between 18 and 65 years old.

Weight

You must be over 50kg.

High Blood Pressure

Acceptable as long as your blood pressure is below 180 systolic (first number) and below 100 diastolic (second number) at the time of donation. Medications for high blood pressure do not disqualify you from donating.

Body Piercing

You must not donate if you have had a tongue, nose, belly button or genital piercing in the past 12 months. Donors with pierced ears are eligible.

Cold and Flu

Wait if you have a fever or a productive cough (bringing up phlegm). Wait if you do not feel well on the day of donation. Wait until you have completed antibiotic treatment for sinus, throat or lung infection.

Diabetes

Acceptable as long as it is well controlled, whether medication is taken or not.

What you should do & description of the process

Online part

  1. You are obliged to read all the requirements if you are looking forward to take part in blood donating
  2. You have to register on our site in order to be qualified to the process
  3. Check your mail to make sure, that your registration was done correctly
  4. That's it!

Donating part

  1. We recommend a small meal prior to donation. It could be a sandwich.
  2. Drink a lot of water.
  3. Go to the adress that you chose with your identity card.
  4. Fill out a questionnaire, which then will be checked and verified by a nurse.
  5. Next, a blood sample will be collected from your fingertip.
  6. You will have an internal medicine examination and interview with a doctor.
  7. If you are qualified, the last part is donating the blood.
  8. Donating the blood (it lasts 5 - 7 minutes).
  9. After donating the blood you will receive a chocolate and a juice.
  10. You are now free to go. You may feel weak and sleepy, make sure that you go back home safely.
  11. You can speed up the regenerating process by having carbohydrate diet this day.
